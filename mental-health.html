Rain Village (Mental Health)

Your mental health matters. Below are resources and ways to seek help.

Depression

Depression is more than just feeling sad. It can affect your daily life and relationships.

  • Mind UK - Depression
  • NHS - Depression
  • Samaritans (24/7 Helpline: 116 123)

Suicide

If you or someone you know is struggling with thoughts of suicide, please seek help immediately.

  • NHS - Support for Suicide
  • Papyrus (Helpline: 0800 068 4141)
  • Samaritans (24/7 Helpline: 116 123)

Self-Harm

Self-harm is a way some people cope with intense emotions. There are healthier methods. Help is available.

  • National Self Harm Network
  • Mind UK - Self-Harm

Anxiety

Anxiety can feel overwhelming but there are strategies and resources to help manage it.

  • Anxiety UK
  • NHS - Anxiety

Bullying

Bullying can have a significant impact on mental health. Support is available.

  • Anti-Bullying Alliance
  • Childline - Bullying

Grief and Loss

Grieving is a natural response to loss. Support is available to help manage during this time.

  • Cruse Bereavement Support
  • NHS - Coping with Bereavement

Stress

Stress is part of life but too much can take a toll. Learn how to manage it effectively.

  • Mind UK - Stress
  • NHS - Stress

PTSD and Trauma

Traumatic experiences can lead to PTSD. Support is available to help with recovery.

  • NHS - PTSD
  • Mind UK - PTSD

Additional Help

Please check out the linked resources.
